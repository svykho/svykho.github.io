About Me

Hi there! I'm Alejandro, a master's student in Advanced Physics and Applied Mathematics at the Universitat de les Illes Balears — but more than that, I’m just a kid still trying to understand the secrets Einstein left behind.

I was born in Madrid, with roots in Ukraine, so I carry a bit of both cultures inside of me. However, it’s the natural world that truly inspires me. I’ve always been drawn to the sea, the mountains, and the quiet beauty of nature — these are the spaces where I find peace and wonder, sparking my curiosity about the Universe.

From a young age, I’ve been captivated by the vastness of space, the mysteries of black holes, and the nature of time itself. My research focuses on perturbation theory, numerical simulations, and the beauty of General Relativity, all in pursuit of understanding how the Universe behaves under extreme conditions. But ultimately, I’m chasing the same questions that have driven so many before me: What happens inside a black hole? How can we truly understand the fabric of the universe?
