Research

My research focuses on black hole perturbation theory, numerical simulations of binary black holes, and gravitational wave physics. I currently work with the Teukolsky equation using the Teuk-Fortran-2020 code, and I am part of the AECT-2025-1-0011 project at MareNostrum 5 to study nonlinear ringdown and wave tails.
